Hi, I'm Archit! I am currently pursuing a research fellowship at Max Planck Institute for Informatics in Germany. Prior to that, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from University of Pune, India.


My long term goals are to understand what intelligence is quantitatively and qualitatively. I wish to holistically study intelligence through various disciplines of knowledge. My immediate goals are to keep on solving interesting problems which align towards my long term goals while regularly dedicating myself for some social cause. Hopefully, this greedy approach makes sense after many years. I thoroughly enjoy and strive to work on challenging problems that have a scope of impact on the research field and humanity at large. I also like working on problems that lie at the intersection of science and computing. I have designed systems, big and small, from scratch and have taken research and technical projects and frameworks from start till completion. Most of my work so far has been in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Linguistics, Knowledge Bases, Information Retrieval and Extraction, High Performance Computing, and Automated Data Processing Pipelines.


I have had the opportunity to work with brilliant individuals in the form of mentors and colleagues. I draw inspiration from them in different ways and I am humbled to have been associated with each one. As part of my immediate future plans, I intend to apply for a Master's degree for Fall 2019.


After this professional talk, let's get to something personal. I am 22 years old and I come from India - the country known for its food, diversity, and spirituality. More specifically I come from Thane (Maharashtra) - the city where one can get the best "Misal Pav" and "Vada Pav" (I may get into trouble over this).


I go by the name "thehippiemonk" on some online platforms. The name suggests the co-existence of two antithetical ideas of being a "hippie" and a "monk". That is metaphorical of the way I strive to attain balance in all spheres of my life. I imagine each such sphere (personal, professional, social, etc.) to be a separate mini-world, where activities occuring within one mini-world should be isolated from the other mini-worlds as much as possible to achieve stability and continuity despite the failure or stagnancy in any one of the mini-worlds.


I have always enjoyed eating and watching good food being prepared. But only recently have I started cooking and thus hacking around with rice and vegetables to make edible enough food. I enjoy running. My personal best has been 5 Km in 29:49 Min i.e. pace of 5:58 Min/Km. I like hiking and mountaineering. I plan on taking a Basic Mountaineering course after returning to India in May 2019.


I was 19 years old when I had my first solo trip to McLeodganj and Dalhousie in Himachal Pradesh. I also had one failed attempt at hitchhiking. I am interested in wildlife photography. I thoroughly enjoy watching movies, documentaries, and cinematographic art.
